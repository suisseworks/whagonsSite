Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Whagons?

    Whagons’ award-winning, cloud-based hospitality software seamlessly combines solutions for reservation management, operational efficiency, revenue management, distribution, guest acquisition, and guest engagement in a single user-friendly system, enhanced by a marketplace of third-party integrations.

  • What does the Whagons Platform do?

    From the front desk to the back office, Whagons allows you to manage your property with one unified platform. More than just a hotel property management system, Whagons’ all-in-one solution includes everything you need to run your day-to-day hotel operations and includes all the functionality of a property management solution, like a reservation system and guest profile management, with so much more. Plus, gain more visibility into your business with reporting and analytics based on your guest data.

  • Who uses the Whagons Platform?

    Whagons’ software solution is designed to power properties of all types and sizes including hotels, hotel groups, hostels, B&Bs and inns, vacation rentals, campgrounds, and more!

  • How much does Whagons cost?

    Whagons offers tiered pricing for independent hotels, properties, and vacation rentals of all sizes and budgets. Get a personalized demo and quote.

  • Does Whagons integrate with other software?

    Via the Whagons Marketplace, you can connect with dozens of third-party software, hardware, and mobile apps using API integrations, including automation services, payment gateways, contactless guest services, check-in kiosks, digital concierges, Point of Sale (POS) providers, and more.

  • Does the Whagons Platform include Hotel management software (HMS)?

    Much more than property management software, the Whagons platform includes everything you need to start, run, and grow your lodging business no matter your property type or size.
